What is 'Think Like a Monk' about?

Jay shetty in his book think like a monk lists down 3 ways to immerse oneself in Monk mindset.

#1 Let Go

Your Identity, Negativity, fear, Intentions

#2 Grow

Finding your purpose, setting up a routine, training your mind and dealing with Ego

#3 Give

first to self in form of gratitude, then giving in relationships and finally to the society
